Students can avail online coaching on following courses

UPSC COMPLETE COURSE

UPSC COMPLETE COURSE is designed to prepare students with the whole syllabus along with several practice sessions and Mock sessions so that they can generate the optimum level of confidence. Suggestive Materials and one-liners are provided for each and every subject and a comprehensive coaching is provided for – Prelims, Mains and Interview (no separate guidance is available for any single subject or level, only complete course is offered so that the faculty members can analyze the student from day one and coach accordingly to cover Prelims and Mains at the same time).

WBCS COMPLETE COURSE

Pabitra Chakraborty, the founder of ClassRoom Academy has made WBCS exam easier with several analytical approaches and by providing students some outstanding tricks to remember each point, understanding the core concept in short time, handling the negative markings and practice methods. The suggestions provided by Pabitra Chakraborty has been playing as the significant key factor behind quick and easy success since 2004. Every year, students are getting more than 85% questions common in both prelims and mains level of WBCS exam from his suggestions.
